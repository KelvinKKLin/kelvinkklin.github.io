Awards and Certificates

Here are a selection of awards and certificates that I’ve received from professional development, work, and participating in programming and engineering competitions.

Licences & Certifications (4 certificates)

Professional Engineer (P.Eng) Licence

Professional Engineers Ontario
Under the Professional Engineers Act, holders of the professional engineering licence from Professional Engineers Ontario are entitled to practice as a professional engineer in the Province of Ontario. To obtain the licence, holders have to fulfill specified academic requirements, obtain a minimum of 48 months of verifiable professional engineering experience, and be of good character.

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)

International Information System Security Certification Consortium
Earners of the CISSP certification have demonstrated their extensive knowledge in key information security domains such as risk management, communications and network security, and identity and access management. They possess the skills needed to design, implement, and manage top-tier cybersecurity programs.

Project Management Professional (PMP)®

Project Management Institute
Earners of the PMP® certification have demonstrated their extensive knowledge and mastery of project management concepts, tasks, and techniques that are applicable across virtually any industry and methodology. They are able to speak and understand the global language of project management, and have demonstrated the knowledge and skills needed to initiate, plan, execute, monitor and control, and close a project.

Certified ScrumMaster (CSM)

Organization: Scrum Alliance
The CSM certification encompassed knowledge of Scrum methodologies, Agile principles, effective team facilitation, and the ability to promote a culture of collaboration and continuous improvement in projects and organizations. It validates expertise in implementing Scrum practices and driving successful Agile transformations.
